Research Paper

Title: Cryptocurrency Volatility Responses to Infrastructure vs Regulatory Events: A TARCH-X Analysis

Author: Murad Farzulla

Affiliation: Resurrexi

DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.17449736

Summary: This study examines how six major cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, XRP, BNB, LTC, ADA) respond to infrastructure events versus regulatory events using TARCH-X models. Analysis covers 50 events from 2019-2025 with multiple robustness checks.
