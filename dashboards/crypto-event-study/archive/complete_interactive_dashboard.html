About This Research

This interactive dashboard presents findings from a comprehensive event study examining cryptocurrency volatility responses to infrastructure and regulatory events. The research analyzes 50 major events across 6 cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Ripple, Binance Coin, Litecoin, and Cardano) from 2019 to 2025.

Key Findings

  • Infrastructure events cause 1.97pp higher volatility than regulatory events (p < 0.001)
  • Cross-sectional heterogeneity spans 2.18pp (ADA: 3.37% vs BTC: 1.19%)
  • 5 out of 6 cryptocurrencies show FDR-significant infrastructure effects
  • Network analysis reveals ETH as most central in spillover effects
  • Regime-switching models show 5x sensitivity amplification during crises

Methodology

TARCH-X models with exogenous event dummies and GDELT sentiment decomposition. Multiple robustness checks including FDR correction, Bayesian inference, and machine learning pattern detection.
